The 3 Goals for the SC Gaming Community

Bring the Gaming Community Together!

Gamers are not supposed to feel alone in the Virtual World when they are Gaming, so the Purpose of The SC Gaming Community is to bring everyone together in one Discord Server so people can play games together.

Game Zone

The SC Community is not about playing one game, there is mutiple games that people play such as Halo, Counter Strike, Minecraft, Roblox, League of Legends, and many more. So gamers should not feel like they are joining a One-Game Only Community.

Make Friends!

When you join the Discord, don't be shy to make friends! Everyone on the community is friendly and kind and the Staff will help you with any problem you have.
